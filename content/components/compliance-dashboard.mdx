## Compliance & Governance Dashboard

A comprehensive compliance monitoring dashboard featuring a donut chart visualization for multi-framework compliance status and governance metrics tracking. Ideal for security, compliance, and audit management applications.

<ComponentCodePreview name='compliancecomponent' />

## Features

- **Multi-Framework Compliance Donut Chart**: Visual representation of compliance across ISO 27001, PCI DSS, NIST, and SOC 2
- **Dynamic SVG Visualization**: Customizable donut chart with color-coded segments
- **Governance Metrics**: Key performance indicators for audit logs and policy updates
- **Compliance Activities List**: Detailed breakdown of governance activities and compliance evidence
- **Responsive Design**: Fully responsive layout with mobile-optimized views
- **Color-Coded Status**: Different colors for each compliance framework
- **Modern Dark Theme**: Professional dark UI with subtle shadows and gradients

## Use Cases

- Compliance monitoring dashboards
- Security operations centers (SOC)
- Audit readiness tracking
- Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC) platforms
- Executive compliance reports
- Regulatory framework management
- CISO dashboards
- Policy management systems

## Frameworks Supported

- **ISO 27001**: Information Security Management
- **PCI DSS**: Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard
- **NIST**: National Institute of Standards and Technology
- **SOC 2**: Service Organization Control 2
